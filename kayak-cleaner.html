Kayaking is a fantastic recreational activity that allows you to explore and appreciate nature while getting a great workout. As you paddle through the water and glide across the surface, your kayak is exposed to various elements like dirt, grime, algae, and even saltwater if you're kayaking in the ocean. Over time, these elements can accumulate and cause damage to your kayak's surface.

Why is KAYAK Cleaner Important?

1. Efficient Cleaning: Regularly cleaning your kayak with KAYAK Cleaner helps to remove all the dirt and grime that accumulates during your kayaking adventures. It is specially formulated to tackle tough stains effectively, leaving your kayak looking brand new.

2. Protection from Rust: If you're kayaking in saltwater, your kayak is at risk of developing rust on its metal components. KAYAK Cleaner not only cleans the surface but also provides a protective layer that prevents rust formation, ensuring the longevity of your kayak.

How to Use KAYAK Cleaner?

Using KAYAK Cleaner is a breeze. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Rinse Off

Start by rinsing your kayak with fresh water to remove any loose dirt or debris. This step ensures that the cleaner can penetrate deeply into the stains for effective cleaning.

Step 2: Apply KAYAK Cleaner

Once your kayak is rinsed, apply KAYAK Cleaner to the surface. Use a sponge or soft brush to scrub the cleaner gently into the stains. Allow the cleaner to sit for a few minutes to work its magic.

Step 3: Rinse and Dry

After the cleaner has had enough time to break down the dirt and grime, rinse your kayak thoroughly with fresh water. Ensure that you remove all traces of the cleaner. Finally, dry your kayak completely before storing it to prevent any moisture-related issues.

1. Prolongs Kayak Lifespan: By regularly cleaning your kayak with KAYAK Cleaner, you are extending its lifespan. The cleaner helps to remove contaminants that could potentially wear down or damage your kayak's surface.

3. Saves Money on Repairs: Neglecting to clean your kayak regularly can lead to expensive repairs down the line. By investing in KAYAK Cleaner and using it consistently, you can avoid these unnecessary repair costs.
