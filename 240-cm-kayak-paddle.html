Q: What is the ideal length of a kayak paddle?

A: The ideal length of a kayak paddle depends on your height, the width of your kayak, and your paddling style. Generally, a paddle between 210 cm and 240 cm is suitable for most kayakers. Q: How do I take care of my kayak paddle?

A: Proper maintenance is essential to prolong the life of your kayak paddle. After each use, rinse it with fresh water to remove any salt or debris. Store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid leaning on or placing heavy objects on the paddle, as this can cause damage. Performing regular inspections and repairs when necessary will help keep your paddle in excellent condition for years to come.
